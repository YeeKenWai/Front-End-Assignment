The Malaysian Indians

Culture

Malaysian Indian culture is rich and diverse, influenced by Tamil, Telugu, Malayalee, Punjabi, and other Indian ethnic groups.

Religion

Malaysian Indians practice various religions including Hinduism, Islam, Christianity, and Sikhism. Hinduism is the predominant religion among Indian Malaysians. Religious festivals and rituals are integral to Indian Malaysian cultural life.

Language

Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, and Punjabi are among the languages spoken by Indian Malaysians, reflecting their linguistic diversity.

Arts and Literature

Indian Malaysian arts encompass classical Indian dance forms such as Bharatanatyam and traditional music genres like Carnatic music. Indian literature, including ancient epics like the Ramayana and Mahabharata, holds significance within the Malaysian Indian community.
