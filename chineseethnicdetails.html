The Malaysian Chinese

Culture

Malaysian Chinese culture is diverse, encompassing various dialects, customs, and traditions brought by immigrants from different regions of China.

Religion

Malaysian Chinese practice a mix of religions including Buddhism, Taoism, Christianity, and Confucianism. Each religion contributes to the cultural practices and rituals within the Malaysian Chinese community.

Language

The Malay language (Bahasa Malaysia) is the national language of Malaysia. It serves as a unifying force among Malaysians of various ethnic backgrounds.

Arts and Literature

Chinese Malaysian arts encompass a wide range of disciplines including calligraphy, painting, music, and dance. Chinese literature, including classical poetry and novels, is valued within the community. Traditional Chinese opera and martial arts performances are also valued forms of cultural expression.
