The Malays (Bumiputra)

Culture

Malays have a distinct cultural identity characterized by customs, traditions, and practices inherited throughout generations, their rich cultural heritage are influenced by other various civilizations including Indian, Chinese, and Arab cultures.

Religion

Predominantly Muslim, Malays follow Islamic practices in their daily lives. Islamic traditions heavily influence Malay culture, including customs related to weddings, funerals, and other ceremonies.

Language

The Malay language (Bahasa Malaysia) is the national language of Malaysia. It serves as a unifying force among Malaysians of various ethnic backgrounds.

Arts and Literature

Traditional Malay arts include dance forms like Zapin and Joget, as well as traditional music such as Gamelan and Keroncong. Their traditional Malay attire includes the Baju Kurung for women and the Baju Melayu for men, often worn during special occasions.
